Sushant Kurren

Travel photographer capturing contemplative moments across the globe. From the highlands of Scotland to the neon-lit streets of Tokyo, documenting the quiet beauty found in movement.

Recent work explores wider cinematic formats and film photography, with a signature teal-amber color palette that reflects the layered compositions of modern travel.

Featured locations span Thailand, Paris, Switzerland, Lofoten, Barcelona, Scotland, Japan, South America, Hong Kong, Lisbon, Cannes, Brussels, Bali, and Sydney.
